Ingredients:
7g fine ground coffee
1 cup of water, preferably high-quality (not tap water)
Machine for Coffee

Process:
Fill the coffee load in your coffee pot attachment. Gently knock it on the table and assemble it.
Whether you have the process on automatic or you control the time, count 25 seconds of dripping into the cup and stop the machine.
Serve it hot and you have your espresso ready!
