Ingredients:
1 ounce of whiskey
1 ounce lemon juice
Half ounce sugar syrup
Soda or mineral water
Lemon slice and cherry

Preparation method:

Add the sugar syrup and lemon to a collins glass. Shake well with the help of a spoon (preferably the special elongated ones).
Put ice in the glass and then fill it with mineral water or soda. Mix a little with the spoon.
Decorate and serve. Add a slice of lemon and a cherry.
