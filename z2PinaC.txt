Ingredients:
30 ml of white rum
90 ml of natural pineapple juice
30 ml of coconut milk
crushed ice
Shaker or blender

Preparation:
Add the white rum, natural pineapple juice, coconut milk and crushed ice to the blender or shaker. 
After shaking it, serve it in the glass .
 It is a sweet drink, so it is not advisable to add more sugar to the preparation. 
 At the end, you can decorate it with a slice of pineapple on the edge.
 Enjoy!
