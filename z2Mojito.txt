Ingredients:
2 ounces of white rum or 60 ml
30 ml of lime juice
Mint leaves
2 tablespoons of sugar
Sprite
crushed ice

Preparation:
Choose a tall glass, then put in the two tablespoons of sugar, the lime juice, a little soda, and the ice.
After stirring, add the shot of rum and a few drops of soda to complete. 
At the end, you can decorate the glass with mint leaves and slices of lime or lemon to make it look better.
