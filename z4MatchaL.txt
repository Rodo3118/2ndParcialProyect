Ingredients:
1 tablespoon of matcha
1/4 cup very hot water
2 tsp of honey
1/2 cup of milk

Preparation:
In a wide cup place the matcha. 
Add the tablespoon of water and whisk vigorously until dissolved.
Add the rest of the hot water and mix again.
 In a saucepan, heat the milk and dissolve the honey in it. 
 Mix the milk vigorously until light foam forms and pour into the cup with the matcha.
 As easy as that, you have your matcha, the only thing left is to enjoy!
