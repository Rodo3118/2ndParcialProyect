Ingredients:
1/2 liter of milk of your preference
3 tablespoons of whole flake oats without cooking or crushing.
2 1/2 tablespoons of Choco Milk in powder

Process:
In the blender, add the oat flakes and the Choco Milk 
Blend for a couple of seconds until you see that the Choco Milk and the oats are mixed.
Add the 1/2 liter of milk of your choice and cover the blender glass. Then blend for 1 minute.
You can add more milk and stir longer if you want a thinner mixture, or add more oatmeal if you prefer it thicker.
Serve the mixture in a glass and enjoy your delicious Choco Milk!
